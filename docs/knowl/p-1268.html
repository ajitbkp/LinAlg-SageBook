Let \(\beta=\{u_1,\ldots, u_n\}\) be an orthogonal basis of an inner product space \(V\text{.}\) Let \(x\) and \(y\) be two vectors such that \(x=\sum x_i u_i\) and \(y=\sum y_i u_i\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation*} \inner{x}{y}=x_1y_1+x_2y_2+\cdots+x_ny_n \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} \norm{x}^2=x_1^2+x_2^2+\cdots +x_n^2\text{.} \end{equation*}
