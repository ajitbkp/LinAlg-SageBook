Checkpoint 7.1.17.

Let \(C([-\pi,\pi])\) be the vectors space of set of continuous functions from \([-\pi,\pi]\) to \(\R\text{.}\) Define the inner product on \(C([-\pi,\pi])\) as

\begin{equation*} \inner{f}{g}:=\int_{-\pi}^\pi f(t) g(t)`dt\text{.} \end{equation*}

Show that under this inner product \(\{1, \sin nx,\cos mx\}\) is an orthogonal set.
