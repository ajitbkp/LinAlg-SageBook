The above system of linear equation can be written as a single matrix equation as

\begin{equation} AX = B\text{,}\tag{1.0.3} \end{equation}

where

\begin{equation} A=\begin{pmatrix} a_{11} \amp a_{12} \amp \ldots \amp a_{1n} \\ a_{21} \amp a_{22} \amp \ldots \amp a_{2n} \\ \vdots\amp \vdots\amp \vdots\amp \vdots\\ a_{m1} \amp a_{m2} \amp \ldots \amp a_{mn} \end{pmatrix}, X=\begin{pmatrix}x_1\\x_2\\ \vdots\\ x_n \end{pmatrix}, B=\begin{pmatrix}b_1\\b_2\\ \vdots\\ b_m \end{pmatrix} \tag{1.0.4} \end{equation}
