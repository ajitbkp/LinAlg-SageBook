  1. \(x \cdot x\geq 0\) for all \(x\in \R^n\text{.}\)

  2. \(x \cdot x= 0\) if and only if \(x=0\text{.}\)

  3. \(x \cdot y=y\cdot x\) for all \(x,y \in \R^n\text{.}\)

  4. \(x\cdot (y+z) = x\cdot y +x\cdot z\) for all \(x,y,z\in \R^n\text{.}\)

  5. \(x\cdot (\alpha y)=\alpha x\cdot y = (\alpha x)\cdot y\text{.}\)
