Theorem 1.5.4.

Suppose the non homogeneous system \(AX = B\) of linear equations has a solution say \(X = X_0\text{.}\) All the solutions of this system are of the form

\begin{equation*} X_0 + X_h, \end{equation*}

where \(X_h\) runs through all the solutions of the corresponding homogeneous system \(AX =0\text{.}\)
