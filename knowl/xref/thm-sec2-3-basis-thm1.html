Theorem 2.4.2.

A set of vectors \(\beta=\{v_1,v_2,\ldots,v_n\}\) is called a basis of \(\R^n\) iff (i) \(L(\beta)=\R^n\)and (ii) \(\beta\) is linearly independent.
