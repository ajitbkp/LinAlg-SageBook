Theorem 7.1.48.

Let \((V,\innprod{.}{.})\) and \((W,\innprod{.}{.})\) be finite dimenional inner product spaces over \(\R\text{.}\) Let \(T\colon V\to W\) be a linear map. Let \(\alpha=\{u_1,\ldots, u_n\}\) be an orthonormal basis of \(V\) and \(\beta =\{v_1,\ldots, v_m\}\) an orthonormal basis of \(W\text{.}\) Then the matrix of \(T\) with respect to these bases is given by
\begin{equation*} [T]_\alpha^\beta=\begin{bmatrix} \innprod{T(u_1)}{v_1} \amp \innprod{T(u_2)}{v_1} \amp \cdots \amp \innprod{T(u_n)}{v_1} \\ \innprod{T(u_1)}{v_2} \amp \innprod{T(u_2)}{v_2} \amp \cdots \amp \innprod{T(u_n)}{v_2} \\ \vdots \amp \vdots \amp \ddots \amp \vdots \\ \innprod{T(u_1)}{v_m} \amp \innprod{T(u_2)}{v_m} \amp \cdots \amp \innprod{T(u_n)}{v_m} \end{bmatrix}. \end{equation*}
